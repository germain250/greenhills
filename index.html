Mission

Green Hills Academy nurtures a community of caring, inquisitive, and principled learners who pursue excellence and contribute to an equitable world.

Core Values

  • Integrity:responsible, respectful, and guided by strong moral principles.

  • Intellectual Curiosity:critical thinker, creative, innovative, and reflective.

  • Inclusivity:caring, collaborative, respectful, and welcoming of diversity.

Why Us?

Full continuum IB world school

Green Hills Academy is Rwanda’s only full continuum IB World School, offering all four IB programmes (PYP, MYP, DP, and CP) to guide students from early years through graduation with a globally recognized education.
